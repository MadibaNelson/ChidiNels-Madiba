This is Voice of Angels Choir

Welcome to Voice of Angels Choir!. This group is dedicated to all the talented individuals who are passionate about music and love to sing. Whether you are a seasoned performer or just starting out, this is the perfect space for you to connect, share your voice and creat beautiful hamrmonies together.

In this group, we encourage a supportive and inclusive environment where members can inspire, moltivate and learn from each other. Heere, you can discuss your favorite songs, share your vocal techniques, seek advise on improving your singing skills and even collaborate on virtual choir projects.

Kindly respect each other's opinions and refrain from any form of negative or criticism. Let's create a vibrant and harmonious community.

Our guidelines are:

  1. Be courteous and respectful: Treat every member with kindness and respect. We value a positive and friendly atmosphere.
  2. Stay on topic: Keep the conversation focused on music, singing techniques, performance tips and related topics. Off-topic discussions should be limited.
  3. No self-promotion: Avoid excessive self-promotion or spamming. However, you are welcome to share relevant performances or achievements.
  4. Avoid excessive forwarding: Refrain from forwarding irrelevant messages or chain messages. Keep the group clutter-free.
  5. Seek help responsibly: If you need assistance or have a query, feel free to ask. However, be patient and avoid respeatedly asking for the same information.


Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in a warning, suspention or expolsion from the choir. The choir executives are avalable to maintain a positive environment and ensure everyone's enjoyment and intrests.

We are excited to have you as a member of the Voice of Angels Choir. Let's share and create extraordinary musical experience together. SING PRAISES!...
